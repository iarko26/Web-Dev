Risotto

Ingredients

  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • water
  • butter
  • onion
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Simmer water and broth in a medium pot
  2. Melt butter in a skillet, add onion and garlic, cook for 5 minutes
  3. Add rice, onion mixture, and salt, cook and toast for 4 minutes
  4. Boil wine in rice mixture, cook for 30 seconds
  5. Cook rice in broth until al dente, then add butter, lemon juice, Parmesan, season, and top with chives and basil

Pasta

Ingredients

  • angel hair pasta
  • water
  • salt
  • onion
  • virgin olive oil
  • Cheese
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Boil salted water with 1 tablespoon
  2. Heat olive oil in a saucepan, add garlic, herbs, and parsley, cook for one minute
  3. Cook angel hair pasta for 2 minutes, add pasta and set aside cooking water. Drain, rinse briefly, and combine with herbed garlic sauce, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper
